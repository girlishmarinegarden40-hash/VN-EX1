After Alice is the first side-story season that continues after the ending of Alice Courtyard. It follows what remains, what leaves, and what is still too unfinished to be named.

Work Statement

This build exists to present the side story in an interactive reading form. Its text, images, music, staging, and interface belong to the project and its creators. Please do not repost, redistribute, mirror, commercialize, extract assets, or republish any part of this build without permission.

Thanks

To every ending that turned out not to be an ending. To every quiet scene that had to be made large enough to breathe. And to every reader willing to follow the garden after the dream has already ended.

Build Note

This is a living side-story build. The novel remains the source, and the staging, audio, CG timing, menus, and presentation can continue to evolve as the world outside the garden becomes clearer.
